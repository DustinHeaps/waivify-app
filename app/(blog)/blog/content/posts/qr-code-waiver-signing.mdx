## Why Use QR Codes for Waivers?

Whether you're hosting a pop-up event, running a yoga class, or taking walk-ins at your tattoo studio — QR codes let clients scan, sign, and go. No more paper forms. No awkward clipboard handoffs.

With Waivify, you can generate a unique link for any waiver and turn it into a QR code in seconds.

---

## What You’ll Need

1. A [Waivify](https://waivify.com) account
2. A published waiver template
3. A free QR code generator (like [qr-code-generator.com](https://www.qr-code-generator.com/))

---

## Step-by-Step: Create a QR Code for Your Waiver

1. **Grab your public waiver link:**  
   Log into Waivify → Go to your template → Click “Share Link”.

2. **Paste the link into a QR generator:**  
   Use any tool (we like [qr-code-generator.com](https://www.qr-code-generator.com/)).

3. **Download your QR code image:**  
   PNG format works great for flyers, signage, or stickers.

4. **Print it or display it anywhere:**  
   Front desk, event signage, product packaging — wherever people can scan.

---

## Best Use Cases

- **Tattoo/Piercing studios**: Let clients scan at the counter while waiting
- **Yoga/Fitness classes**: Print on a poster at the door
- **Events/Workshops**: Put on name badges or table tents
- **Mobile services**: Show the QR on your phone or business card
